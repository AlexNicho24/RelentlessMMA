David Carter has adequately trained in various martial arts for over 10 successful years ranging from Muay Thai, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Savate Thai X-Training, Combat Submission Wrestling, Freestyle Wrestling, Jeet Kune Do Concepts, Filipino Martial Arts, Shooto, and Jun Fan Gung Fu among others, unique styles proven effective over the years on the street, and in such events as the Ultimate Fighting Championship, and its Japanese counterpart Pride FC. A formidable competitor and champion in kickboxing, submission grappling, and mixed martial arts events. Dave has, in addition, battle-tested all of his effective techniques in the corporeal world as a security specialist and consultant since 1997 when he started handling security for parties in college and was later asked to work all over DC and VA in clubs and bars for his security, personal protection, and resolution skills. With all of this, he undoubtedly possesses a unique understanding of what works in the ring and what works on the street. David has discovered what does and doesn't work inside the gym, how to manage weight for competitions and life, and get the utmost out of his training that he conveys to his clients. His students are from all walks of life from teenagers, college students, stay at home mothers, software engineers, law enforcement, all branches of the military, the disabled and Senior citizens.
